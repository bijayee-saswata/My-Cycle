Most advanced bike sharing solution. Beautiful & functional bicycles powered by GPRS, GPS and Bluetooth technology.It has a secure and hasslefree digital wallet to pay.
Get Started

Locate Bike

Most likely you will see one near you,  in case you dont, locate an available bike using Cyclenta app.

Scan QR Code

Click Unlock icon and scan the QR code on the bike. Bike will open automatically.

Ride 

Enjoy your bike ride. Take to office, shopping or just for fun. Follow rules and look after your safety.

Park and Lock

Park the bike at Cyclenta Zone only, the lock will close automatically, and press end ride in the App. We will deduct the money from your wallet.
